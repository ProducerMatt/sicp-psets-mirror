6.001 Spring 1998: (Non) Problem set 5

Quiz 1 announcement

Issued Tuesday, March 3, 1998

Quiz 1 is on Wednesday, March 11. The quiz will be held in Walker Memorial Gymnasium (50-340) from 5--7PM xor 7--9PM. You may take the quiz during either one of these two periods, but students taking the quiz during the first period will not be allowed to leave the room until the end of the period. Optional review sessions for the quiz will be held on Sunday night, March 8, from 7--10 PM in rooms 34-301, 302, 303, and 304.

The quiz is closed book, but you may bring with you one sheet of notes that you have prepared to help you study, and also this handout (with additional notes written on the handout if you like). The quiz will cover material presented from the beginning of the semester through recitation on March 6, the textbook through section 2.5.2, and the general material covered in the problem sets through problem set 4.

The quiz will be based on a sample implementation of a system for housing incoming freshmen in Metropolitan Storage Warehouse. This code is available here.

You should get a copy of the actual handout distributed in lecture on Tuesday, March 3, and bring a copy of it with you to the quiz. For students who miss lecture, extra copies may be obtained from the 6.001 course secretary, Rebecca Bisbee in NE43-430.
